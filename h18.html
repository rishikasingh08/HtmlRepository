LIGHTHOUSE

A lighthouse is a tall building that sends out light for use in navigation. Lighthouses are built on the coast of an ocean or lake. The lighthouse protects ships from crashing into shore, by sending the light out towards the sea. ... T he light is usually covered by a Fresnel lens.
